About Me

I am a PhD student at the University of Saskatchewan, Canada. Currently I am working on articulatory speech synthesis which focuses on the generation of high quality and expressive synthetic speech using a 3D model of the human vocal tract.

My research interests include signal processing, machine learning, applied mathematics, speech synthesis and computational biomechanics.

I hold an MS degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Ulsan, South Korea, and a BS degree in Electronic Engineering from the International Islamic University Islamabad.
